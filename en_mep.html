MEP Engineering

Integrated engineering solutions for power, HVAC, and fire fighting systems.

The Lifeline of Modern Buildings

MEP works are crucial for the efficient operation of any facility. At MGM, we provide comprehensive solutions starting from precise design and engineering calculations, through supply from top global brands, to installation, Testing, and Commissioning.

We have extensive experience in Central HVAC systems (Chillers, AHUs, VRF), executing massive projects like Dabaa Nuclear Plant and Alamein Towers. We guarantee energy efficiency and maximum thermal comfort.

Scope of Work:

  • Electrical Works (MV & LV).
  • HVAC Systems (All types).
  • Fire Fighting Networks (Sprinklers, FM200, CO2).
  • Plumbing and Water Treatment.
  • Light Current Systems (CCTV, Data, Sound).
