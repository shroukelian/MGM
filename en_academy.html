Environmental Consultancy

Sustainable Solutions for a Green Future

At MGM, we recognize the importance of sustainability. Our Environmental Consultancy division provides studies and practical solutions to help companies and factories comply with local and international environmental laws (ISO 14001).

Our team of environmental experts is ready to provide technical support in environmental risk assessment and developing management plans for major projects.

Our Environmental Services:

  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA).
  • Solid and Hazardous Waste Management.
  • Air quality, Noise, and Emissions measurements.
  • Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy solutions.

Training Academy

Empowering Engineering & Technical Workforce

MGM Academy aims to bridge the gap between theoretical study and practical application. We offer intensive training programs designed specifically for engineers and technicians to enhance their efficiency and keep up with the latest market technologies.

Training is conducted by elite consultants with field experience, providing practical workshops and accredited certificates.

Key Training Programs:

  • Project Management Professional (PMP).
  • Health, Safety & Environment (OSHA/NEBOSH).
  • Technical workshops in MEP and Maintenance.
  • Engineering Design Software (Revit, AutoCAD).
